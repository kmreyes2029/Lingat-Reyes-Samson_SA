About the Authors

It's more fun in Zamboanga

This website was created by a group of passionate students dedicated to showcasing the beauty, culture, and unique flavors of Zamboanga. Driven by curiosity and creativity, the authors worked together to design an informative and visually engaging platform that celebrates the region’s history, traditions, and local delicacies. Through careful research, writing, and layout planning, they aimed to provide visitors with a meaningful online experience that inspires appreciation for Zamboanga’s vibrant identity. Their collaboration reflects not only teamwork and skill, but also a genuine desire to share knowledge and highlight the richness of Philippine culture.
